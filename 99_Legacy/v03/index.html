About Us

Established in 2024 and nestled in Crowhill, in the picturesque Borrowdale valley, The Hill Preparatory School is more than just a school, it is a transformative educational journey whose aim is to unlock the full potential of every child.

At The Hill, our curriculum goes beyond the traditional focus of English, Maths and Science. Here pupils embark on an exploration of the Oxford curriculum in Computing, Global Skills Projects, Sustainability and Wellbeing – enabling them to develop critical thinking, creativity, ethical decision-making and environmental stewardship – while enjoying the dream of learning “Frui Somnium Doctrina”.

Our experienced teaching staff are led by the Headmaster, Mr Hans Christen, the Deputy Headmaster, Mr Tex Harding, The Head of the Infants’ Department, Mrs Pamhidzai Chikumba and the Director of Sport, Mr Graeme Ellis.

Core Values

Academic
Rigor

We are commited to upholding the highest standards of academic excellence, leveraging cutting edge resources and exceptional faculty.

Whole Child
Development

We believe in cultivating the intellectual, emotional, physical and social well-being of our students, empowering them to be self-aware, resilient and adaptive individuals.

Global
Mindset

We forster an international perspective, equipping our students with the knowledge, skills and cultural understanding to navigate and succeed in the interconnected world.

Ethical
Leadership

We instil the values of integrity, empathy, sustainability, social responsibility, preparing our students to become ethical decision-makers and agents of positive change.

Innovation and
Creativity

We celebrate and encourage innovative thinking, problem solving and creative expression enabling our students to pioneer solutions to the challenges of the future.

Inclusive
Community

We celebrate diversity and build collaborative community that respects and embraces individual differences, preparing our students to thrive in an increasingly diverse world.
