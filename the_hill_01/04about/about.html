The Hill

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Borrowdale valley, in Crowhill, The Hill Preparatory is more than just a school, but a transformative journey that unlocks the full potential of every student.

Established in 2024, this elite institution is redefining the prowess with the cultivation of well-rounded, globally-minded leaders.

At The Hill, our curriculum goes far beyond the traditional focus on math, science, and English. Here, students embark on a holistic exploration of the world, developing essential life skills like critical thinking, creativity, ethical decision-making, and environmental stewardship.
